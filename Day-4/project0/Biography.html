Vijay Deverakonda Biography



Vijay Deverakonda is an Indian film star known for his works in Telugu films. Vijay made his film debut in 2011 in Ravi Babus romantic comedy Nuvvila. However, Vijay gained recognition with his supporting job in Yevade Subramanyam.



Date of Birth 9 May 1989
Occupation Indian Film Actor
Years Active 2011-present
Known For Works in Telegu Cinema
Parents Deverakonda Govardhan Rao, Deverakonda Madhavi
Education Badruka College Of Commerce And Arts, Little Flower Junior College
Siblings Anand Deverakonda
Awards Filmfare Award for Best Actor-Telugu
Residence Hyderabad, Telangana, India
